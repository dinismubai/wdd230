Description of the audience

This website is aimed at traders as well as companies. The audience of this website is interested in finding business opportunities and connecting to a reliable and functional network of merchants spread all over the province. Furthermore, this website is directed to all persons or organizations who wish to do business clearly and transparently and in compliance with the law.

  3. Who are the ideal partners for the type of business I do?
  4. What's new in the business world?
  5. Up-to-date information on business procedures.
