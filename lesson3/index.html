Site Name

Maputo Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to be a bridge for all individuals as well as companies to find business exchange opportunities. This initiative aims to create a hub where entrepreneurs of all levels can find tailor-made partnerships. It is intended to provide an integrated network of information that will allow traders to make calculated decisions about how and where to invest. On this website, you will find all the statutes and laws on National and International trade as well as facilities in all negotiations that are made.
